Things Chingford Homeowners Want To Know About Loft Insulation

Is 270mm loft insulation always necessary?

This is the current UK recommendation for optimal thermal performance. In most cases, reaching this depth provides the best balance of efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

Can I still use my loft for storage after insulation?

Yes, but adjustments may be needed. Raised boarding systems are often used to allow storage while maintaining insulation depth underneath.

Does insulation need to be replaced regularly?

Not usually, but older insulation can lose effectiveness over time if it becomes compressed, disturbed or affected by moisture.

Will insulation make all rooms warmer?

Insulation improves overall heat retention, which helps maintain a more consistent temperature throughout the property.

Is loft insulation a disruptive job?

Most installations are completed with minimal disruption, especially when access to the loft is straightforward.
